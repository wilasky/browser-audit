Two tools. One extension.

◆

Browser Health Check

16 security and privacy checks against a maintained baseline. Get a score, see what's wrong, and fix it with a single click — the button takes you directly to the right Chrome settings page.

⌖

ScriptSpy

See what JavaScript is actually doing on any website. Network requests, cookie access, mouse tracking, canvas fingerprinting — all attributed to the script that caused them, with a risk score.

🔒

Privacy by design

Everything runs locally. No browsing history, URLs, or personal data ever leaves your device. ScriptSpy only activates when you open the popup — no background monitoring.

⌘

Fingerprint entropy

Measure how unique your browser is for passive tracking. Canvas, WebGL, audio, navigator properties — all combined into a bits-of-entropy score with actionable guidance.

⊟

Extension audit

Check all your installed extensions against a blacklist of known malicious extensions. Flag extensions with excessive permissions. Detect sideloaded or Manifest V2 extensions.

↓

Export reports

Download your audit as a structured JSON file or a formatted PDF. Keep records of your security posture over time with the built-in score history chart.
